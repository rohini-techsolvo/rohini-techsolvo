Faq's

frequently asked questions

1. What is Brainy Ladder?

Brainy Ladder is a new age startup working towards the promotion of early learning for kids by doing and exploring. We believe that children are naturally curious and love to play. We combine the two with various activities which offer a do-it-yourself approach. Children can perform the activities, with/under the guidance of their parents; which will offer a sense of accomplishment and they will also learn in that very process. All Brainy Ladder products come with videos and instructions which are easy for the parents to follow while they explore the product with their child.

2. What do I get inside a Brainy Ladder Kit?

Each kit for different age group comes with 300+ activities to understand the connection between various concepts. These kits come with daily plans, revision based materials and all items required for hands-on learning.

The kit will help your child to construct various models for understanding the fundamental principles and applications of various concepts. These kits will be delivered to your doorstep.

3. Are Brainy Ladder products safe for my kids?

LBrainy Ladder products are made for very young children, and their safety is our top most priority. All our products in the kit are completely safe for use by children.

4. Can my child do these activities independently?

The usage of kits will require the involvement and/or supervision of an adult for children below 5 years. These kits are curated for spending quality time between parent and a child also.

5. Who designs the materials?

Kits by Brainy Ladder are designed by pedagogy experts, child development professionals and early learning experts from some of the most top research centers known for foundation of early learning. You can be assured that all the products and materials received by you are tested, of highest quality, child-friendly and are curated age specifically.

6. What age groups is Brainy Ladder kit available for?

At Brainy Ladder we have designed kits age specifically. The kits are available for 1.5-2.5 years old, 2.5-3.5 years old, 3.5-4.5 year old & 4.5-5.5 years old. We also have a kit for Playgroup 3years to 8years Children's called Phonics.
